Payment and Donations

Thank you so much for your interest in the DIY Healer’s Flea!

We require vendors and healers to send payment in order to reserve space at the next DIY-HF. As an approved applicant, you should use this page to pay for your space via the service of your choice. Please follow the instructions regarding how to pay carefully to ensure the fastest reservation possible.

How to Pay

Follow these instructions carefully to ensure your payment is successul and your reservation is processed in the shortest amount of time.

  1. Determine how much you need to pay; refer to our price list for details.
  2. Click a “Pay via…” payment link from the payment options below. (Sorry, we only accept payments in the ways linked from this page; no personal cheques.)
  3. Complete the payment form; you will be returned to this page when your payment is successful and has begun to process.
